

# Ishav Sohal — Personal Portfolio Website

## Overview
A bold, colorful, single-page portfolio website that showcases who you are as a developer, your projects, work experience, and how to get in touch. The design will use vibrant gradients, strong accent colors, and eye-catching section transitions to create an engaging, memorable experience.

---

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Hero Section
- Large animated greeting with your name: **Ishav Sohal**
- Tagline: something like *"Full-Stack Developer · Systems Thinker · Builder"*
- Brief 2-3 sentence intro drawn from your background (UofT CS, passion for building reliable systems, hackathon enthusiast)
- Links to GitHub, LinkedIn, and email as icon buttons
- Subtle animated gradient background

### 2. About Me
- A short, personable paragraph about who you are — your interests, what drives you (building elegant solutions to real-world problems, curiosity about AI, love of moving fast)
- Tech skills displayed as colorful pill/badge tags grouped by category (Languages, Frameworks, Cloud/DevOps, Tools)

### 3. Projects
Four project cards with bold visual styling:
- **Bitly Clone** — Distributed URL shortener (Docker, Redis, Cassandra)
- **r/Place Clone** — Real-time collaborative canvas (AWS, Redis, WebSockets)
- **FaceVote** — Identity-verified voting app (DeerHacks 2023)
- **CarbonTrack** — Mobile carbon footprint tracker with gamification

Each card will include:
- Project name and tech stack tags
- 2-3 bullet point highlights
- GitHub link (placeholder, you can add real links later)

### 4. Experience
- Timeline-style layout for your role at **Environment and Climate Change Canada**
- Title, date range, and key accomplishments as bullet points
- Education section with UofT, GPA, and Dean's List recognition

### 5. Contact
- Simple section with a call-to-action message
- Email link, LinkedIn, and GitHub icon buttons
- Optional: a simple contact form (sends to your email)

---

## Design & Interaction Details
- **Color palette**: Bold gradients (e.g., purple-to-blue, orange-to-pink accents) on a light or off-white base
- **Typography**: Modern sans-serif, large headings with gradient text effects
- **Animations**: Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins for each section, hover effects on project cards
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly layout
- **Navigation**: Sticky top navbar with smooth-scroll links to each section
